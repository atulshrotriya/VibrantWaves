Our Story

We are to create a compassionate community dedicated to mental health advocacy, educating in the art of meaningful self-care routines, and the de-stigmatization of prioritizing well-being.

Our Mission

We focus on children and young adults’ mental wellness needs, promoting healing, community awareness and inclusion and otherwise contributing to the local community with the goal of providing a unique approach to channel feelings, emotions, stress and trauma through artistic mediums such as painting, sculpting, poetry, creative writing, music, and photography. We offers various creative activities including but not limited to art contests, visiting art museums and specific art shows, decorating facilities with client art, professional art instruction, and presenting art pieces.
